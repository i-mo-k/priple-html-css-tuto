Synopsis

The story takes place in 2001 in the fictive city of Karakura. Ichigo Kurosaki, 15 year-old teenager, is able to see, hear, and touch wandering souls of dead people since he was a child. One evening, his everyday routine gets messed up when he meets Death God Rukia Kuchiki, and encounters a monster, called hollow. As the hollow is desperately trying to feed his hunger for humans souls by devouring Ichigo's relatives, Ichigo seeks help from Rukia and accepts her offer to become a Death God by himself.

Characters (in development)

  • Ichigo Kurosaki


    Ichigo Kurosaki (黒崎 一護, Kurosaki Ichigo) is a Human who is also a Substitute Shinigami. Ichigo is the son of Isshin and Masaki Kurosaki, and older brother of Karin and Yuzu. He is also married to Orihime Inoue and has a son named Kazui Kurosaki.
